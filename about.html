ABOUT MY JOURNEY

Hi, I'm NYAKAISIKI MONICA, a passionate web developer with a love for creating beautiful and functional websites. My journey in web development began THREE months ago when I first discovered the magic of building things on the web.

I started my path with basic HTML and CSS, creating simple static websites. The thrill of making my own designs come to life was exhilarating, and soon I was diving deeper into JavaScript, exploring how interactive elements could improve the user experience. With each new project, my knowledge expanded, and I found myself more excited about learning.

I’ve had the opportunity to work on diverse projects, from responsive websites to full-stack applications, and I’m always eager to take on new challenges.

I’m constantly improving my skills through courses, projects, and collaborating with other developers. My focus is always on writing clean, maintainable code and providing a smooth user experience. I strive to stay up-to-date with the latest trends and best practices in the industry.

When I’m not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or reading tech blogs and attending meetups. I’m always excited about the next project and look forward to helping bring your web development ideas to life.
